How to Use

  • Hover over any point to view system details
  • Click a point to pin the tooltip (click × to close)
  • Click "View Paper" in tooltips to access reference
  • Drag to pan around the sky map
  • Scroll to zoom in/out
  • Use controls to change projection, point size, and color scheme
  • Use slider to step through discoveries in each year
